About

Bob Ross was an influential painter, inspiring people of all artistic skill levels to take up painting. He is commonly known for his tv series The Joy of Painting . During its 31 season air time, viewers fell in love with Bob's cathartic voice and demeanor.

In his early life, Bob Ross was enlisted in the military, acheiveing the rank of Master Seargeant. During his term in Alaska, he gained inspiration from the grand mountains and landscapes that were right in his backyard. From this inspiration, and his practice in liquid-based oil painting techniques, Bob Ross shared his passion and trade with everyone through his show The Joy of Painting

The Joy of Painting was extremely successful running for 31 seasons, in which Bob showed viewers a step-by-step process on how to create absolutely marvelous landscape paintings in a single session. With knowledge of composition, perspective, and clever use of tools, he showed that anyone could make truly beatuiful paintings.

Bob Ross passed away on July 4, 1995 at the age of 52.
